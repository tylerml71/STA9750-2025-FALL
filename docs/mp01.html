Exploring the Most Popular Programming on Netflix

New York, NY, September 28, 2025: During this past decade, viewers have been showing more and more interest in both foreign and non-English films like the award-winning film Parasite, and the massively popular show Squid Games (both currently offered on Netflix). To keep up with this growing demand, Netflix has invested and expanded globally, partnering with companies and studios to add high-quality films and shows to its offerings.

This week, K Pop Demon Hunters, Wayward, She said Maybe, and Billionaires’ Bunker are leading the top 10 on its’ platform for English movies, English shows, non-English Movies and non-English Shows, respectively. Netflix continues to dominate the top 10 on its’ platform this week as every #1 title is a Netflix Original. This leads to some further questions about Netflix and their performance between its English and non-English content. Even though all of these films and movies are at #1 in their category, how popular are they compared to each other? And is Netflix seeing non-English content outpacing English content in terms of growth and viewership?

Aquiring Data

In order to answer these questions, we will use the language R to set up and analyze our findings. First, let’s prepare the foundation to answer these important questions. There are two files we will use to analyse and find our answers, from Netflix’s TuDum Top 10. The two files are Global Top 10 and Country-wide Top 10, both important for finding popularity of films and shows on a large and smaller scale. We will use multiple libraries to analyze as well, such as tidyverse, knitr, and DT.

Everything looks good at an initial glance. However, it’s always important to make sure that the formatting within a data set is correct, or there can be issues. These missing values (commonly known as NA or Not Available) do not follow the formatting that R detects. We can replace “N/A” or “NA” strings with proper NA values either by mutating Adds or modifies columns in a data frame by computing new variables. the column after import or specifying them when reading the file.

Show the code

Currently, it operates in 94 different countries.1 However, the streaming platform did cease its operations in Russia, exiting the market on February 27, 2022, for a total of only 35 weeks this year.2

Despite this, global content is still proving to be very successful. For example, the top global non-English film is All Quiet on the Western Front, an anti-war film set in World War 1. # Question 5, Code to calculate longest uninterrupted weekly run in the top 10
COUNTRY_TOP_10 |>
  filter(category == 'TV') |>
  arrange(country_name, show_title, week) |>
  group_by(country_name, show_title) |>
  mutate(
    week_diff = as.numeric(week - lag(week)),
    group_id = cumsum(ifelse(is.na(week_diff) | week_diff != 7, 1, 0))
  ) |>
  group_by(country_name, show_title, group_id) |>
  summarise(successive_weeks = n(), .groups = "drop") |>
  group_by(country_name, show_title) |>
  summarise(longest_successive_run = max(successive_weeks), .groups = "drop") |>
  slice_max(longest_successive_run) |>
  rename(
    `Country` = country_name,
    `Title` = show_title,
    `Longest Successive Run (Weeks)` = longest_successive_run
  ) |>
  kable("html", caption = "What TV show had the longest uninterrupted weekly run in the top 10?") |>
  kable_styling(bootstrap_options = c("striped", "hover", "condensed", "responsive"), full_width = F)

Because of this, it takes more hours to finish a TV show compared to films and will have heavily inflated numbers. Stranger Things: Dominating the past and the future as Season 5 Approaches

New York, NY, October 1st, 2025: The show Stranger Things has been one of Netflix’s most popular shows worldwide, consistently ranking among Netflix’s most watched shows since its introduction in 2016. Across its first four seasons, the series has achieved a total of 2.97 Billion hours watched, with fans clamoring for season five its sure to rise even more.9 Stranger Things also spent almost half a year in the top 10 in 2022(as shown below), and has had clear global success with season 4. It even sparked newcomers to join in on the release of season 4, with seasons 1, 2, and 3 all reaching the top 10 at the same time as season 4 for three months. Overall, Stranger Things has such a strong presence and viewership, beating Wednesday, Bridgerton, and Inventing Anna.
